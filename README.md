# Blinkit-Sales-Performance-Analysis
An end-to-end Power BI project analyzing $1.2M in grocery sales data to identify consumer trends, outlet performance, and inventory optimization for Blinkit.

🛒 Blinkit Sales Performance Analysis
Comprehensive Analysis of India's Last-Minute Delivery App Sales Data

📌 Project Objective
To perform an end-to-end analysis of Blinkit’s sales performance, focusing on consumer preferences, outlet efficiency, and product distribution using Power BI.

📊 Business Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
Total Revenue: $1.20 Million

Average Order Value: $141

Total Items Sold: 8,523

Customer Satisfaction: 3.9 / 5.0 Average Rating

🛠️ Technical Workflow
Data Cleaning: Handled inconsistent labeling in the "Item Fat Content" column (Standardized low fat, LF, and Low Fat into one category).

Standardization: Grouped outlets into Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 for geographical comparison.

DAX Modeling: Developed custom measures for Average Sales and Item Count distribution.

Visualization: Designed a high-fidelity dashboard featuring:

Doughnut Charts for Fat Content analysis.

Stacked Column Charts for Outlet establishment trends.

Funnel Maps for regional sales distribution.

💡 Key Insights
Health Trends: 64.7% of customers prefer Low Fat items over Regular.

Regional Leaders: Tier 3 locations contribute the highest revenue ($0.47M).

Top Categories: Fruits, Vegetables, and Snack Foods are the top-selling product lines.

Outlet Efficiency: Supermarket Type 1 is the most consistent performer in terms of volume and ratings.
